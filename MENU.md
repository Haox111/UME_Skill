## 定制选项 Customization

**甜度 Sugar:** 0% / 25% / 50% / 75% / 100%
- Note: Tiramisu Matcha Latte cannot be 0% sugar; Coco Pistachio sugar is not adjustable.

**冰量 Ice:** Regular / Light ice / No ice / Hot (hot available for select milk teas only)

**杯型 Size:** Regular 500ml / Large 700ml / Mega 1000ml (select items only)

> First time? Try 50% sugar — most drinks are quite sweet at full sugar.
